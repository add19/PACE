This year we have...

Neetigya-“य ुती सव ेठः”

“Get yourself acquainted to everything you need to know about politics this Election Season.” Have you ever wondered how election campaigns work? Get to know about how a political party works right from its initial phase to its victory phase. It will include various exciting rounds that will make you feel to be in actual political party.

Qriosity-The globe of scruple

Have you watched game shows like ‘Kaun Banega Crorepati’ or ‘Kya aap paanchvi pass se tez hai?’ and felt that you wanted to participate in such competitions? Well, here is your opportunity! Qriosity is an event designed as a quiz show testing the participants’ general knowledge and mental agility. It will include various exciting rounds that aim to create the atmosphere of the quiz game shows. As a participant, you will get to know different types of quizzes conducted and you can also test your knowledge via this competition. Overall, this is an event which you will thoroughly enjoy.

Ideathon-Marathon of Ideas

Ever wondered what goes into transforming your idea into reality? Ever wanted to solve real world problems with innovative solutions? Introducing the first ever ideathon in Walchand College of Engineering, Sangli. An ideathon is an experience for students to address some of the most pressing challenges of our time. You would get a chance to work in teams, use design thinking and innovative learning practices to ideate and collaborate on possible solutions. Improve your analytical thinking, problem solving, logical thinking, foresight and decision making through this one of a kind experience.

Voclash-Words form Worlds!

War is not the solution to every problem, Stand up and debate. This is an event which primarily focuses on debate. Participants will learn different kinds of debates, they will also get to know how to speak formally. The rounds are made in such a way that focuses on important aspects of debating skills
